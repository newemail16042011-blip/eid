For Zanib

First of all… I'm already sorry, Zanib, for saying all this here. But I had a small hope in my heart, so I wished you.

I know you're not mine anymore. You belong to someone else now, and I truly respect that. But it has been two years and I still haven't been able to move on… maybe I never will. I tried twice, but it didn't work. And honestly, now I don't even want to try anymore.

I know none of this probably matters to you. Maybe you even hate me now… maybe while reading this you're thinking it's all nonsense, or maybe you're even cursing me for disturbing you. And if you feel that way, I understand.

Maybe you don't love me anymore… but the truth is, I still do.

I also know you will probably never come back to me, because you already have someone in your life who is better than me, someone who makes you happier than I ever could. And I truly hope he always keeps you happy.

But even knowing all this… somewhere in my heart, I'll still be here, quietly waiting. Not to disturb you, not to force anything… just with the same feelings that never really left.
